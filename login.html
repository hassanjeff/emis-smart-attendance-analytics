EMIS Section · Directorate of Education, GB

The EMIS Section exists to turn scattered district attendance sheets into one clear, real-time record — giving the Directorate a single source of truth across all ten districts.

Our Mandate

This system replaces manual spreadsheet merging with centralized data entry — cutting turnaround time from days to minutes, and removing the errors that come with copying numbers by hand.

Efficiency

Consistent attendance data doesn't just report the past — it flags patterns early, before a few missed school days become a dropout risk.

Early Warning

District Deputy Directors see their own district clearly. Director EMIS and Deputy Director EMIS see the full picture across all ten. Same data, the right view for every role.

Role-Based Access

Reliable, up-to-date attendance data is one of the clearest signals the Directorate has for deciding where support and resources are needed most.

Resource Allocation

Education systems worldwide are shifting from static year-end reports to real-time monitoring. This system brings that same standard of visibility to SED GB.
